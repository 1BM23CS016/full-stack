Classic Pancakes

This classic pancake recipe is fluffy, easy to make, and perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Instructions:

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. Add milk and egg; stir until smooth.
  3. Cook on a hot skillet until golden brown.
